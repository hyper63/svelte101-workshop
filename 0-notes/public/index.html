SvelteJS 101 Workshop

Welcome

Welcome to the SvelteJS 101 Workshop, this is a three hour workshop, where we will cover the following topics: setup, basics, reactivity, async, stores, actions, and testing.

Instead of the feature tour style approach, this workshop will work through simulated lessons and exercises to cover real-world like use cases. This should give you a perspective on how all of the features fit together in a real application, without having to build full applications from scratch.

The workshop has a lot to cover, in a short amount of time, and we want to be respectful of everyone's time. With that said it is an immersive course, so you will have the opportunity to actively participate in the workshop with some exercises at the end of lesson. You don't have to finish the exercises and the workshop is designed so you can complete the exercises at a later time. After the exercise period the instructor will go over their answer to at least the first exercise in the lesson, to give you some feedback.
